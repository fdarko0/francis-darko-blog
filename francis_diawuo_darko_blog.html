About Me

Welcome to my blog! My name is Francis Diawuo Darko. I am a researcher focusing on social integration experiences of young migrants in Ghana. My work aims to understand and improve the lives of these individuals through detailed research and practical solutions.

Profile

I have presented my research at various conferences, including the Development Futures Conference organized by the Development Studies Association of Australia. My studies revolve around the informal economy in Sunyani, Northern Ghana, and the challenges faced by young migrants in integrating into this sector.

Research Activities

My current research focuses on the manuscript titled "Journeys of Belonging: Social Integration Experiences of Young Northern Ghanaian Migrants in Sunyani's Informal Economy". I explore the social dynamics and integration strategies of these migrants, aiming to provide insights that can inform policy and support systems.
